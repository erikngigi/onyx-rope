Onyx Rope is a simple, distraction-free interval timer built for skipping rope workouts.

Whether you're warming up, doing HIIT, or pushing through endurance rounds, Onyx Rope keeps you on track with a clean and intuitive interface.

<b>Features</b>

<ul>
  <li>Set your active (jump) duration in seconds</li>
  <li>Set your rest (cooldown) duration between sets</li>
  <li>Choose the number of sets for your session</li>
  <li>Audio cues to signal transitions between jump and rest phases</li>
  <li>Screen stays on during your workout so you never lose your place</li>
  <li>No ads, no accounts, no internet required — fully offline</li>
</ul>

<b>Who is it for?</b>

Onyx Rope is perfect for anyone who skips rope as part of their fitness routine — from beginners building stamina to athletes using jump rope as a serious conditioning tool.

<b>Privacy</b>

Onyx Rope collects no data whatsoever. No analytics, no crash reporters, no third-party SDKs. Your workout stays on your device.
